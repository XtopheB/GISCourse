Protected Areas / Japan

A sample dataset of 10 protected natural areas across Japan, used in SQL training exercises. It covers parks, forests, wetlands, urban green spaces, and nature reserves — with attributes suitable for practicing filtering, sorting, grouping, and joining operations.

Data dictionary — protected_areas
id integer
Unique numeric identifier for each feature. Used as a primary key when joining tables.
Examples: 1210
name text
Full name of the protected area in English. Each name is unique within the dataset.
Examples: Kushiro WetlandsShinjuku Gyoen
type text
Category of protected area. One of five possible values representing the land management classification.
Values: ParkForestWetlandUrban greenReserve
area_ha integer
Surface area of the protected area in hectares (ha). Ranges from small urban parks to very large national reserves.
Range: 38 ha → 226,764 ha
est_year integer
Year the protected area was officially established. Useful for temporal filtering and ordering queries.
Range: 1917 → 2007
region text
Administrative region of Japan where the area is located. Acts as a foreign key linking to the regions table.
Values: HokkaidoKantoChubuKyushuOkinawa
